Introduction

A gasket is a sealing element used to prevent leaks of fluids, gases, or particles between two surfaces. Made from materials like rubber, metal, or plastic, it ensures a tight, secure fit in various systems.

ZNI Gaskets Products

We offer three main types of gaskets:
1. Non-Metallic – For general-purpose sealing in low to medium pressure applications
2. Semi-Metallic – Combination materials for improved performance in high pressure/temperature
3. Metallic – For extreme pressure & temperature conditions

Spiral Wound Gaskets

Premium Semi-Metallic Sealing Solution

Spiral wound gaskets consist of a metallic winding strip and a soft filler material wound together in a V-shaped spiral configuration. This unique design provides exceptional resilience and sealing capability under extreme conditions.

Temperature Range -200°C to +1000°C
Pressure Rating Up to 250 bar
Materials SS304, SS316, Graphite
Standards ASME B16.20, EN 1514-2

Key Features & Benefits

  • Excellent recovery properties under thermal cycling
  • High blow-out resistance due to spiral construction
  • Suitable for both raised face and RTJ flanges
  • Minimal gasket stress relaxation
  • Available with inner and outer centering rings

Common Applications

  • Oil & Gas Processing - High pressure pipelines and refineries
  • Petrochemical Industry - Reactors and heat exchangers
  • Power Generation - Steam turbines and boilers
  • Chemical Processing - Aggressive chemical environments
  • Offshore Platforms - Marine and subsea applications

Metal Jacketed Gaskets

Superior Protection for Extreme Conditions

Metal jacketed gaskets feature a soft filler material completely encased in a metallic jacket. This construction provides excellent chemical resistance and mechanical protection while maintaining sealing effectiveness.

Temperature Range -200°C to +800°C
Pressure Rating Up to 150 bar
Jacket Materials SS316, Inconel, Hastelloy
Filler Options Graphite, PTFE, Ceramic

Key Features & Benefits

  • Metallic jacket protects filler from chemical attack
  • Excellent for corrosive and erosive media
  • High structural strength and blow-out resistance
  • Suitable for damaged or irregular flange surfaces
  • Custom jacket designs available (single, double, corrugated)

Common Applications

  • Chemical Plants - Highly corrosive environments
  • Pharmaceutical Industry - Sanitary applications
  • Heat Exchangers - High thermal cycling conditions
  • Valve Applications - Bonnet and body joints
  • Pipeline Systems - Flanged connections in harsh conditions

Ring Type Joint (RTJ) Gaskets

Metal-to-Metal Sealing for Critical Applications

RTJ gaskets are solid metal rings designed to fit into specially machined grooves in flanges. They provide metal-to-metal sealing for the most demanding high-pressure and high-temperature applications.

Temperature Range -200°C to +1200°C
Pressure Rating Up to 1000 bar
Ring Styles R, RX, BX, AX, IX
Materials SS316, Inconel, Monel

Key Features & Benefits

  • Superior sealing performance at extreme pressures
  • Metal-to-metal contact ensures leak-tight seal
  • Ideal for high-temperature hydrocarbon service
  • Self-energizing design under pressure
  • Compliant with ASME B16.20 and API 6A standards

Common Applications

  • Oil & Gas Wellheads - High-pressure drilling operations
  • Offshore Production - Subsea equipment and risers
  • Refineries - Critical high-pressure connections
  • Pressure Vessels - ASME code applications
  • High-Pressure Piping - Class 600 and above flanges

Non-Metallic Gaskets

Versatile Sealing for General Applications

Non-metallic gaskets offer excellent flexibility and conformability, making them ideal for low to medium pressure and temperature applications. Available in various materials to suit different service conditions.

Temperature Range -50°C to +400°C
Pressure Rating Up to 40 bar
Materials PTFE, Rubber, Graphite
Thickness 0.5mm to 6mm

Material Options

  • PTFE (Teflon) - Excellent chemical resistance, low friction
  • Compressed Fiber - Cost-effective, general purpose
  • Rubber (NBR, EPDM, Viton) - Flexibility and resilience
  • Graphite - High temperature resistance, low stress
  • Non-Asbestos - Environmentally friendly alternatives

Common Applications

  • Water & Wastewater - Pumps and valves
  • HVAC Systems - Ductwork and equipment flanges
  • Food & Beverage - Sanitary connections
  • General Industry - Low-pressure piping systems
  • Automotive - Engine and transmission applications

CamProfile Gaskets

Advanced Semi-Metallic Sealing Technology

CamProfile gaskets feature a serrated metal core with soft facing material on both sides. This design combines the strength of metal with the sealing ability of soft materials for superior performance.

Temperature Range -200°C to +700°C
Pressure Rating Up to 200 bar
Core Material SS304, SS316, Titanium
Facing Graphite, PTFE

Key Features & Benefits

  • Serrated profile provides multiple sealing lines
  • Excellent for uneven or warped flange surfaces
  • Lower bolt loads required compared to flat metal gaskets
  • Superior blow-out resistance
  • Reusable in many applications after inspection

Common Applications

  • Heat Exchangers - Shell and tube connections
  • Pressure Vessels - Manway and nozzle connections
  • Petrochemical Plants - Process equipment
  • Power Plants - Steam and condensate systems
  • Pharmaceutical - Clean steam applications

Kammprofile Gaskets

High-Performance Serrated Metal Gasket

Kammprofile gaskets feature concentric grooves machined into a solid metal core, with soft coating on both sides. This design ensures excellent sealing even under thermal cycling and mechanical stress.

Temperature Range -200°C to +850°C
Pressure Rating Up to 250 bar
Core Material SS316L, Inconel 625
Coating Expanded Graphite, PTFE

Key Features & Benefits

  • Concentric serrations provide multiple sealing barriers
  • Excellent recovery after thermal cycling
  • Low creep relaxation properties
  • Minimal maintenance and long service life
  • Compliant with TA-Luft, EN 1092-1, and ASME B16.21

Common Applications

  • Chemical Processing - Reactors and distillation columns
  • Power Generation - Turbines and boiler connections
  • Refineries - High-temperature hydrocarbon service
  • Pharmaceutical - Clean and sterile environments
  • Food Industry - Hygienic processing equipment
